Welcome, I am

Lorenzo

CIPRIANI

28 years old, born and raise in Rome.

Since I was a child I've always been passionate about IT. Videogames and the need to assemble increasingly powerful PCs represented the drive towards the study of hardware and software components.

After graduating in political science and working in a wide variety of jobs in order to achieve economic independence, I decided it was time for me to try something new, so I left for Dublin, an experience that led me to profound personal and cultural growth.

The return to Rome and the need to change my life, brought me closer to the world of web development, a committment intesified by the loss of my job due to the lockdown. I embarked on the learning of the main CMS and I started to realize websites as a freelancer. Thereafter I realized that this is what I want to do with my life and I started studying the tools I needed to build my career as a web developer.

And this is just the beginning...
